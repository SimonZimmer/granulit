    • Granulit is a Düsseldorf, Germany based producer and DJ who currently discovers new musical terrain in experimental techno.
    His signature sound intertwines mystic harmonies and archaic drums with heavily processed field recordings yielding abstract motives.
    In a conflict of both intuitive and algorithmic gestures, complex and strange atmospheres emerge, generating an obscure acoustic scenery

    The debut EP "undreamed" was released in october 2015 on Black Hills Recordings.

    As a participant in the founding of the event series Vertigo, Granulit curates a wide spectrum of electronic music within the Düsseldorfer club-scene.
    In 2015, he founded the Dark-Wave live-project Exolar in collaboration with guitarist Yannik Brehm.
