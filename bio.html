    • BIOGRAPHY:

    Granulit is a Düsseldorf, Germany based producer and DJ who currently discovers new musical terrain in experimental techno.

    His signature sound intertwines mystic harmonies and archaic drums with heavily processed field recordings yielding abstract motives.
    In a conflict of both intuitive and algorithmic gestures, complex and strange atmospheres emerge, generating an obscure acoustic scenery.
    The debut EP "undreamed" was released in october 2015 on Black Hills Recordings.

    In 2017, he joined Haut und Knochen, a creative collective consisting of musicians, designers and art directors, set out to combat the mainstream and revitalize the subculture as an inindependent event series.
    As a participant in the founding of the event series Vertigo, he further curates the spectrum of electronic music within the Düsseldorfer club-scene.


    Granulit is engaged in several collaborative efforts to branch out into other musical territories.
    At the moment, he's working on the following projects:

    The Dark-Wave live-project Exolar, exploring intersections between Techno and New Wave within the framework of a live performance.
    Granulit: sequencer, laptop, vocals and self-made controllers
    Guitarist Yannik Brehm: guitar, sequencer, synthesizer.

    A conceptual album as an attempt to fuse technologically driven electronic music and nature-inspired black metal, with the singer and film-maker Ozan Tasci

    An experimental EP with the Producer/DJ Kaream, pursuing old- and new school Electro Aesthetics
